Spaghetti

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Tomato paste
  • 20ml of Oil

How to make it:

  1. Turn the stove, put water in a pot until it reaches the boiling temperature
  2. After starting boiling put 500g of pasta in it
  3. Let it there until the pasta becomes soft
  4. When done get rid of the water and let it rest for 10 mins
  5. Put 20ml of oil in the pan
  6. Put 200g of tomato paste let it there for 3 mins and then put the pasta
  7. Let it cook for about 15 mins
  8. Turn of the stove and let it rest for another 15 mins
