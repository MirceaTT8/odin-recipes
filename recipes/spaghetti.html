Spaghetti:

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes pasta
  • 20ml of oil
  1. Boil 500g of pasta for 20 mins
  2. After boiling let the pasta rest for 15 mins
  3. Put 250g of tomato paste in the pan and let it simmer for 5 mins
  4. Then put the pasta in let it there for 15 mins
  5. Let the food rest again for 15 mins and it's done
